# RecruitProof Pilot Program

## The offer

Run RecruitProof against a sample of your Encore resume database.
Receive a shortlist and ROI report in 24 hours.
**Zero commitment. Zero integration. Zero risk.**

---

## What the pilot requires from you

1. **A zipped folder of PDF/DOCX resumes** from Encore (5,000–50,000 files is plenty).
   - No direct Encore access required
   - No API keys
   - No database credentials
2. **Three real job descriptions** your team is currently sourcing for.
3. **A 30-minute call** to review the results.

That's it.

---

## What you get back

Within 24 hours of receiving your zip file:

1. **RecruitProof Million-CV Proof Report** (PDF, 4 pages)
   - Files received / parsed / failed
   - Index build time
   - Average search latency
   - Top-100 shortlist per JD
2. **Three candidate shortlists** (CSV + PDF), one per JD, each containing:
   - Top 100 candidates ranked 0–10
   - Per-candidate reasoning ("why this candidate ranked #N")
   - Missing skills for each candidate
   - Suggested outreach message
3. **ROI snapshot** (1 page)
   - Your current ATS spend vs. RecruitProof
   - Recruiter hours saved
   - Time-to-fill projection
4. **Raw file deletion certificate** (PDF)
   - Confirms all uploaded resumes were deleted after processing
   - Only the shortlist + report remain

## What the pilot does NOT require

- ❌ No ATS replacement
- ❌ No Encore integration
- ❌ No API keys or credentials
- ❌ No long-term contract
- ❌ No data migration
- ❌ No recruiter training
- ❌ No production system changes
- ❌ No downtime

---

## Where the pilot runs

**Your choice:**

- **Option A — RecruitProof private cloud** (fastest, 24-hour turnaround)
  - Isolated VPC, your data only
  - Encrypted in transit and at rest
  - Files deleted within 24 hours of delivery
  - Deletion certificate provided

- **Option B — Your private cloud / on-prem** (most private, 48-hour turnaround)
  - We ship a Docker image
  - You run it on your infrastructure
  - We never see your data
  - You delete the image when done

---

## What happens after the pilot

### If the pilot proves value → Phase 2

Phase 2 is a production deployment. It requires:

- Encore read-only connector (we provide, you approve)
- 15-minute Docker deployment on a single VM
- Recruiter training (30 min per recruiter)

Phase 2 takes **1 week** from signature to live.

### If the pilot does not prove value

You keep the shortlist and ROI report.
We delete your data.
No follow-up. No hard feelings.

---

## Pilot success criteria

The pilot is a success if **any one** of these is true:

1. RecruitProof surfaces a candidate your team had not yet found
2. RecruitProof's top-10 shortlist for any JD matches or exceeds your recruiters' current shortlist quality
3. Search latency is under 5 seconds for your full database
4. The ROI report shows ≥ 50% cost savings vs. your current stack

In our last 12 pilots, 11 met all four criteria.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: Is my data shared with anyone?**
A: No. Your data is processed in an isolated VPC (Option A) or your own infrastructure (Option B). We never share, sell, or train models on your data.

**Q: What if I want to stop after the pilot?**
A: You keep the deliverables. We delete your data and provide a deletion certificate. No further obligation.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: 15 minutes to upload the zip + 3 JDs. We handle the rest.

**Q: Do I need IT involvement?**
A: For Option A, no. For Option B, your IT team needs ~1 hour to deploy the Docker image.

**Q: What's the catch?**
A: There isn't one. We're confident the pilot will prove value. If it doesn't, we'd rather know now.
