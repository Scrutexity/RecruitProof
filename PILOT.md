# RecruitProof Pilot — For Rudy

## The goal

Scan your Encore export (5,000–10,000 resumes) and surface the top candidates
for a given job description.

## How it works

1. **Export**: You export a ZIP of resumes from Encore (PDF/DOCX).
2. **Scan**: I run RecruitProof locally. It parses files, extracts text, generates embeddings, and ranks candidates.
3. **Shortlist**: You get a ranked list with scores (out of 10) and reasoning for each candidate.
4. **Scale**: If valuable, we index your full archive.

## What RecruitProof does

- **Parses PDFs and DOCXs** — tested on synthetic datasets; ready to run on yours.
- **Deduplicates** candidates by email, phone, and content hash.
- **Ranks** using 5 signals: semantic match, role-fit, skills, behavioral, career trajectory.
- **Explains** why each candidate scored what they did, and what they're missing.
- **Runs locally** — no data leaves your machine. No cloud. No API calls required.

## What RecruitProof does NOT do

- **Store or share your data** — the scan runs locally. No data leaves your machine.
- **Replace Encore** — this is an intelligence layer, not an ATS replacement.
- **Write to your ATS** — all connectors are read-only by design.
- **Require a long onboarding** — we can run the scan within an hour of receiving your export.

## What's been tested vs. what's projected

**Tested (real):**
- Engine works on 10,000 synthetic resumes (27-second index build, 50ms warm search).
- 32 unit tests pass (ranker, JD parser, hybrid retrieval, end-to-end search).
- PDF/DOCX extraction works on real files.
- Deletion certificate is generated by a real script (`delete_raw_files.py`).
- The code is MIT-licensed and fully auditable on GitHub.

**Projected (not yet measured on real customer data):**
- 500K-resume scale (extrapolated from 10K benchmark; linear scaling expected).
- Extraction success rate on real Encore exports (depends on file quality).
- Recruiter-rated shortlist quality (no recruiters have reviewed output yet).
